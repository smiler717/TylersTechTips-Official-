Building Your First PC

Published on April 14, 2025 by Tyler

Building your first PC can be a rewarding experience, and it’s easier than you think! In this guide, we’ll walk you through the essential steps to assemble a budget-friendly rig.

Step 1: Gather Your Components

You’ll need a CPU, motherboard, RAM, storage (SSD/HDD), GPU, power supply, case, and cooling. Stick to a budget of $500–$800 for a solid starter build.

Step 2: Assemble the Core

Install the CPU on the motherboard, add the RAM, and secure the motherboard in the case. Be gentle with the CPU pins!

Step 3: Add Storage and Power

Mount your SSD/HDD, connect the power supply, and cable-manage to keep things tidy. A clean build helps with airflow.

Step 4: Boot and Install

Connect a monitor, power on, and install your OS (like Windows or Linux). Update drivers, and you’re ready to go!

Enjoy your new PC, and don’t hesitate to upgrade as needed!
